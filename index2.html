Liz

Liz and I had some pretty separate interests before we started dating. I liked gaming and she liked painting. Now that we’re dating, I still like gaming, but I also like a bunch of other things like art, writing, museums, travelling (kind of) and eating new things.

She’s also changed, as she still likes painting but also gaming and driving and taking trips to museums. We’ve changed each other’s political views quite a bit, and we agree on a lot of things in life.

My girlfriend, ultimately, has brought me from past to present and from uncertainty to happiness.
